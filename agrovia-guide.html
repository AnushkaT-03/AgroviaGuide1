Role 1 — Farmer

Farmers don't need an app or login. Just arrive at the warehouse with your produce — the Operations Admin handles all digital intake on your behalf.

ℹ️
No App or Login Needed. The warehouse admin registers your harvest, runs quality grading, and the system auto-calculates your payout — fully transparent, no middlemen.
Your 4-Step Journey
1
Register with Agrovia (One-Time Only) ▼
First-time farmers provide their name, farm location, and bank details to the warehouse admin. The admin registers you in the system. Your name then appears in the "Select registered farmer" dropdown for all future deliveries — you only need to do this once.
💡 After registration, you're in the system permanently. No repeat paperwork.
2
Deliver Produce to the Warehouse ▼
Bring your harvested produce in crates to any Agrovia warehouse — Mumbai, Pune, or Satara. The admin handles all digital intake from this point. No forms, no app, no hassle on your side.
3
Wait for Quality Grading ▼
Your produce is physically inspected and assigned a grade (A, B, or C). The grade directly determines your payout rate — higher grade = higher Rs./kg rate. Pricing is fully transparent and visible on the platform.
GradeQualityPayout
APremium QualityHighest Rate
BStandard MarketStandard Rate
CBelow StandardLower Rate
4
Receive Direct Payment ▼
Once the grade is confirmed, your payout is processed directly to your registered bank account. No middlemen, no deductions. The exact Rs./kg rate is tied to your grade.
🔒 Always remind warehouse staff to log out of shared devices after registering your delivery to protect your bank details.
🏭

Role 2 — Operations & Warehouse Admin

You are the hub of the supply chain — registering every harvest, grading every batch, and managing all inventory from intake to dispatch.

🔑
Login: Go to agroviaa.vercel.app → Select Operations Management → Enter the pass-key below.
Pass-Key
PB2806
Section A — Farmer Intake
1
Open Farmer Intake▼
From the left sidebar, click Product Management, then select the Farmer Intake tab. This opens the New Harvest Registration form.
2
Fill in the Harvest Registration Form▼
Complete all required fields:
  • Product — choose crop type (Cauliflower, Tomato, Lettuce, etc.)
  • Farmer — select from dropdown, or use + to register a new farmer
  • Harvest Date — the actual date of harvest (required)
  • Target Warehouse — Mumbai, Pune, or Satara
  • Quantity (kg) — total weight delivered
  • Storage Method — Normal Storage or Cold Storage
3
Submit — Batch Created!▼
Click Submit / Register Harvest. The system instantly generates a unique Batch ID (e.g. BTH-MMM8ANF9-L27E) and a QR code.
🏷️ Print and attach the QR code label to the physical crates immediately. The batch status is set to Test Pending.
4
Proceed to Quality Testing▼
On the batch confirmation screen, click "Proceed to Quality Testing" — this takes you directly to the Quality Reports section with the new batch pre-loaded.
Section B — Quality Reports
5
Open Quality Reports & Select a Batch▼
Click Quality Reports in the sidebar. All batches with Test Pending status appear in the list. Select a batch by Batch ID or farmer name.
6
Grade the Batch▼
Physically inspect the produce and assign one of three grades:
GradeDescription
APremium Quality
BStandard Market Quality
CBelow Standard
🔬 AI scan is available for demo — in live operations, physical test results determine the grade.
7
Confirm the Grade & View Pricing▼
Click Confirm Grade. The system displays the full pricing breakdown:
  • Farmer Payout — Rs./kg paid to farmer
  • Warehouse Price — retailer purchase price
  • Retailer Selling Price — customer price (incl. testing & logistics)
Section C — Warehouse Inventory
8
Current Inventory Tab▼
Shows all batches in stock across Mumbai, Pune & Satara. Filter by Fresh / Consume Soon / Expired. Scan or type a Batch ID to look up any crate — full tracking details and AI suggestions appear on the right panel.
📦 Top stat bar shows: Total Batches · Fresh Stock · Low Shelf-Life · Total Crates.
9
Live Logistics Tab▼
Monitor real-time delivery status of all dispatched batches — in transit, delivered, or any exceptions.
10
Crate Management Tab▼
Track physical crates — which are at the warehouse, which are with retailers, and which need collection and return.
11
Orders Tab — Fulfill Retailer Orders▼
When a retailer places a restocking request, it appears here. Review the order, select matching inventory batches, and confirm dispatch. The retailer's kiosk inventory updates automatically.
🏪 Orders are location-tied — Pune kiosk orders are fulfilled from Pune Warehouse stock.
🔒
Always log out when leaving a shared terminal — click the profile avatar (top-right) → Logout. The dashboard contains farmer payment records and full inventory data.

Manage your Agrovia retail kiosk — accept stock from the warehouse and bill customers through the built-in POS system.

Tab A — Customer Billing (POS)
1
Open Customer Billing (POS)▼
From the Retailer Portal header, click Customer Billing (POS). Two panels appear: left for customer & item entry, right for the live bill.
2
Enter Customer Details▼
Type the customer's phone number in the Customer Phone field.
  • ✅ Existing Member — name and ID auto-appear with a PREMIUM badge. Discounts apply automatically.
  • 🆕 New Customer — click Create Member ID to register them on the spot.
3
Add Items to Cart▼
Use the dropdown to Scan QR or select a batch from kiosk inventory — or type the Batch ID directly. Enter the Quantity (kg) and click the blue + Add to Cart button.
📦 "Out of Stock" items need restocking first — go to Tab B (Inventory Procurement).
4
Review Bill & Checkout▼
The right panel shows a live bill with each item, Batch ID, weight, and price. For members, the discounted Total is shown in blue. Select Payment Method (Cash or other), then click Checkout.
🧾 A bill is generated with a unique code and QR. Hand the bill to the customer — they use the code to verify their purchase in the Consumer portal.
Tab B — Inventory Procurement
1
Place a Warehouse Order▼
Under Place Warehouse Order, select your supply warehouse (e.g. Pune Warehouse), pick the Product Batch from available stock, and enter Quantity in crates.
2
Choose Payment Option & Submit▼
Select Pay Instantly or Pay Later from the payment dropdown, then click Submit Order.
🏭 The Operations team fulfills your order from their dashboard — stock appears in your kiosk inventory once dispatched.
3
Track in Recent Orders▼
The right panel shows all orders with their Order ID, crate count, source batch, warehouse, and payment status. Orders update to Fulfilled once the warehouse dispatches.

Role 4 — Consumer

Verify your purchase, trace produce origin, manage your membership, and get support — all from the Consumer portal.

What You Can Do
1
Verify Your Purchase▼
On the Customer tab, enter your unique bill code (from the kiosk receipt) and click the search icon. Your purchase details and product quality info appear instantly.
🧾 The 8-character code is printed at the bottom of your receipt, near the barcode.
2
Scan the Bill QR Code▼
Tap Scan QR Code (top-right of the Customer tab) and point your camera at the QR on your receipt. It auto-fills and verifies your code. Zoom in on the QR for best results.
3
Trace Your Produce▼
Click the Traceability tab and enter a Batch ID (found on your receipt or product label, e.g. BTH-MMM8ANF9-L27E). See the complete journey: farmer origin, harvest date, quality grade, warehouse entry, and kiosk delivery.
4
Join as a Member▼
Click Membership in the sidebar, select a plan, and register with your phone number. You'll get a unique Agrovia Member ID — just give your phone number at any kiosk checkout for automatic discounts.
5
Get Help via the Chatbot▼
See the chat bubble icon in the bottom-right corner of the portal? Click it for instant answers on membership, traceability, kiosk locations, and billing. No waiting, no phone calls.
🤖 The chatbot is rule-based — fast, accurate, and designed for the most common queries.
🔒
Always log out via the profile icon (top-right) when done — especially on shared kiosk terminals or public devices. This protects your Member ID and purchase history.

🔍 How QR Traceability Works

Every crate gets a unique QR-coded Batch ID. Here's the full lifecycle — from field to your phone.

🌾
1 — Farmer Arrival
Produce is logged with farmer ID, crop type, quantity, and harvest date.
🔬
2 — Quality Test
Standardised inspection is run; grade and test results are recorded in the digital audit trail.
🏷️
3 — QR Code Generated
Unique Batch ID + QR code is printed and applied to each approved crate.
📦
4 — Storage & Dispatch
Crate is tracked through warehouse storage and outbound dispatch to retail.
🏪
5 — Kiosk Receipt
Retailer scans to confirm receipt; kiosk inventory updates automatically.
📱
6 — Consumer Scan
Buyer scans QR to view full origin, quality grade, and farm story. No app needed — works in any smartphone browser.
📱
No app download required. Any smartphone camera pointing at an Agrovia QR code will open the trace page in-browser instantly.
